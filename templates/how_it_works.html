How Splitsy Works

Factory-sealed units. Bulk prices. No membership. No repackaging. Ever.

The Core Rule

🔒 Sealed Units Only — Always

Every item on Splitsy must be factory-sealed in its original, untouched packaging — exactly as it left the store shelf. No repackaging. No handling. No guesswork. If it's sealed, it's safe.

Six Simple Steps

🛒

1. Seller makes a bulk purchase

Shop at Costco, Sam's Club, Amazon Bulk, or another bulk retailer. Buy only factory-sealed multi-packs.

📋

2. Seller lists individual sealed units

Scan the item, upload the receipt, set a per-unit price within the markup cap, and select a public meetup spot.

🔍

3. Buyer browses nearby listings

Filter by neighborhood community, product category, or bulk store. Verified prices and seller ratings visible before claiming.

💳

4. Buyer claims and pays

Claim one or more units and pay securely in-app. Payment is held in escrow until the exchange is confirmed.

🤝

5. Exchange at a public spot

Meet at a pre-designated public location — building lobby, park, or community center — for a quick handoff of sealed units.

⭐

6. Both parties rate the exchange

Ratings keep the community honest. Low-rated sellers surface less prominently in search results.

Two Ways to Split

✅ Pioneer Model

Seller buys first, lists units immediately. Buyers get instant availability — no waiting.

Best for: Non-perishables, household goods, personal care

🤝 Pool Model

Post a deal, buyers commit and pay upfront. Purchase happens once the group is complete.

Best for: Higher-cost items where seller wants guaranteed demand

How We Keep It Fair

🧾

Receipt Verification

Sellers upload receipts. Splitsy auto-calculates cost and enforces markup cap.

🔒

Sealed Units Only

Every listing must be factory-sealed. Flaggable by buyers. No exceptions.

🛡️

Markup Cap

Prices capped at a regulated % above cost. Splitsy stays a savings platform.

🚩

Community Flagging

Buyers flag anything off. Platform reviews within 24 hours.

⭐

Ratings System

Both parties rate after each exchange. Accountability built in.
